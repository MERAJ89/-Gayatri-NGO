About Our Organization

Gayatri Social Welfare Foundation is a registered NGO dedicated to uplift communities and create positive social change. Founded in 2025, we work tirelessly to support those in need across Paschim Bardhaman, West Bengal.

Our mission is to build a compassionate society where every individual has access to basic necessities, education, and opportunities to thrive.

Learn More

Our Focus Areas

Support the Poor

Providing basic necessities and financial support to underprivileged families in our community.

Education

Ensuring access to quality education for children from disadvantaged backgrounds.

Food Security

Distributing food and organizing nutrition programs for vulnerable populations.

Healthcare

Facilitating access to affordable healthcare and medical assistance.

Shelter

Providing safe housing and support programs for homeless individuals.

Skill Development

Training programs to enhance employability and create economic opportunities.
